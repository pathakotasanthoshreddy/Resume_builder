ATS Resume Best Practices

Follow these guidelines to ensure your resume passes through ATS

Avoid These

  • Tables and columns
  • Headers and footers
  • Images and graphics
  • Text boxes and shapes
  • Uncommon fonts
  • Creative layouts

Always Include

  • Standard section headers
  • Clear job titles
  • Company names and dates
  • Bullet points (• not fancy symbols)
  • Relevant keywords
  • Simple formatting

Pro Tips

  • Use PDF format (most ATS read it well)
  • Include keywords from job description
  • Spell out acronyms first time
  • Use standard month/year date format
  • Save as "FirstName_LastName_Resume.pdf"
  • Test with free ATS scanners online
